# Stormy Weather (A Love Song)

**Verse 1**  
Thunder rolls across the midnight sky,  
Rain taps a rhythm on the window pane.  
Lightning paints your silhouette nearby,  
In the hush, I whisper your name.

**Pre-Chorus**  
Clouds may gather, winds may cry,  
But in your arms, the world feels right.

**Chorus**  
Stormy weather, wild and untamed,  
But your love is my shelter, my flame.  
When the night is cold and the sky is torn,  
I find my calm in the eye of your storm.

**Verse 2**  
Shadows dance as the tempest grows,  
You pull me close, we sway in the dark.  
Outside, the river of thunder flows,  
Inside, I’m safe in the spark.

**Pre-Chorus**

**Bridge**  
Let the rain fall, let the thunder sound,  
We’ll weather it all, love knows no bounds.  
Every flash of light, every trembling night,  
I’ll hold you close till the morning is bright.

**Outro**  
In the hush after rain, when the dawn is new,  
I’ll still be here, holding on to you.
