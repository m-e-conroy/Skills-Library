# When the Sun Goes Down
*A Ballad of Endings*

---

## Verse 1
We stood beneath the fading sky  
The day was breathing its last breath  
You couldn't look me in the eye  
As colors bled toward their death  
The crimson clouds like roses torn  
Scattered petals cross the blue  
I knew our love would not see morn  
The way I knew I'd always love you  

## Verse 2
The sun sank slow behind the hill  
Just like the words you couldn't say  
The world grew cold, the air grew still  
As golden light slipped away  
Each shade of orange, red, and gold  
Was one more memory burning bright  
Our story ending, growing old  
Surrendering to coming night  

## Chorus
When the sun goes down on love  
There's beauty in the letting go  
Like fire painted high above  
That dies in one magnificent show  
We had our moment in the light  
We burned as bright as we could burn  
But even suns give way to night  
And some things never will return  

## Verse 3
I watched the shadows stretch and grow  
Across the fields where we once walked  
You turned away, I let you go  
There was nothing left to be talked  
The horizon swallowed up the day  
The first star pierced the darkening dome  
And just like sunlight fades away  
I watched you disappear toward home  

## Chorus
When the sun goes down on love

Like fire painted high above  
That dies in one magnificent show  
We had our moment in the light

## Bridge
They say tomorrow brings new light  
That dawn will break the spell of sorrow  
But I'll be thinking of tonight  
And not about tomorrow  
This twilight hour is ours alone  
The ending of our sacred story  
Where love once lived, now silence grown  
One last glimpse of fading glory  

## Verse 4
So let the darkness have its way  
Let night fall soft upon this land  
We cannot hold another day  
Cannot turn back time's cruel hand  
But in that final golden hour  
Before the sun completely dies  
I felt the beauty, felt the power  
Of loving you beneath these skies  

## Final Chorus
When the sun goes down on love  
There's grace in watching it descend  
Like fire painted high above  
All beautiful things must end  
We had our season in the sun  
We loved as deeply as we knew  
And though our day together's done  
I'd watch it set again with you  

## Outro
The last light fades, the stars appear  
The night is gentle, dark, and deep  
And though you're gone, I'll hold you here  
Until the world has gone to sleep  

---

**Performance Notes:**
- Tempo: Slow, melancholic (♩ = 65-75 BPM)
- Key suggestion: E minor or A minor for mournful tone
- Instrumentation: Fingerstyle guitar, cello or upright bass, subtle harmonium
- Vocal style: Tender, resigned, with quiet intensity in the bridge
- Dynamic arc: Start gentle, build through bridge, return to quiet reflection in outro
