# Golden Morning Light
*A Folk Song*

---

## Verse 1
The sun breaks over the eastern hill  
Painting the sky in amber and gold  
The morning air is quiet and still  
Like stories my grandmother told  
Of simpler days and warmer nights  
When love was young and hearts were true  
And every dawn brought new delights  
I see them all when I see you  

## Chorus
Oh, the sunrise brings them back to me  
Like ghosts upon the morning wind  
Those faces I can barely see  
Those voices calling once again  
In every ray of golden light  
I hear their laughter, feel their warmth  
The sunrise holds them tender-tight  
Before the day comes rushing forth  

## Verse 2
My father's hands were strong and worn  
From working soil and tending fields  
I think of him each breaking morn  
And all the wisdom that he wielded  
My mother's song still fills the air  
When dewdrops glisten on the grass  
She taught me how to love and care  
For fleeting things that cannot last  

## Chorus
Oh, the sunrise brings them back to me  
Like ghosts upon the morning wind  
Those faces I can barely see  
Those voices calling once again  
In every ray of golden light  
I hear their laughter, feel their warmth

## Bridge
We danced beneath the summer stars  
We sang around the firelight  
Time has taken them so far  
But mornings make the distance right  
For just a moment, they're all here  
Standing in that gentle glow  
Their spirits whispering so clear  
The love I'll always know  

## Verse 3
Now I stand here all alone  
Watching colors fill the sky  
But I am more than flesh and bone  
I carry them, they'll never die  
In every dawn that paints the earth  
In every bird's awakening song  
I remember all their worth  
And know that I still belong  

## Final Chorus
Oh, the sunrise brings them back to me  
Like ghosts upon the morning wind  
Those faces I can barely see  
Those voices calling once again  
In every ray of golden light  
I hear their laughter, feel their warmth  
The sunrise holds them tender-tight  
My memories, my precious gold  

---

**Performance Notes:**
- Tempo: Moderate, contemplative (♩ = 80-90 BPM)
- Key suggestion: D Major or G Major for warmth
- Instrumentation: Acoustic guitar, light fingerpicking, optional fiddle or mandolin
- Vocal style: Gentle, reflective, building emotion in the bridge
